Specializing in precision cutting, creative coloring techniques, and bespoke styling, Ronaldo takes pride in tailoring each service to enhance his clients' unique features and personal style.

Senior Hairstylist & Colourist

Anna specializes in cutting, styling, coloring, highlights/lowlights, balayage, color correction, keratin treatments, and hair up styles for special occasions.

Her meticulous attention to detail and commitment to understanding each client's unique needs have earned her a dedicated following of satisfied clients who trust her expertise and creative vision.
