Visiting Highgate

Art Hair & Beauty is located in the heart of Highgate Village, one of London's most charming and historic neighborhoods. Known for its beautiful architecture, green spaces, and village atmosphere, Highgate offers a peaceful retreat from the bustle of central London.

Parking

Limited pay-and-display parking is available on Highgate High Street and surrounding roads. We recommend using public transportation when possible.

Public Transport

Highgate Underground Station (Northern Line) is approximately a 10-minute walk from the salon. Several bus routes also serve the area, including routes 143, 210, and 271.

Nearby Attractions

While you're in the area, you might enjoy visiting Highgate Cemetery, Waterlow Park, or one of the many historic pubs and cafes in Highgate Village.
