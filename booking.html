Booking Information

Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans can change. If you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, please give us at least 24 hours' notice. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a cancellation fee.

Late Arrival Policy

If you're running late, please let us know as soon as possible. We'll do our best to accommodate you, but late arrivals may result in a shortened appointment to ensure we stay on schedule for other clients.

First-Time Clients

For first-time clients, we recommend arriving 15 minutes early to complete a consultation form and discuss your hair goals with your stylist.

What to Expect

Your appointment will begin with a thorough consultation to understand your needs and preferences. Our stylists will provide professional recommendations and ensure you're completely satisfied with your service.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my appointment?

We recommend booking 1-2 weeks in advance for regular appointments and 3-4 weeks for special occasions to ensure you get your preferred time slot and stylist.

Can I request a specific stylist?

Yes, you can select your preferred stylist when booking online or mention your preference when booking by phone or email.

How long will my appointment take?

Appointment duration varies depending on the service. Haircuts typically take 45-60 minutes, while color services can take 2-3 hours or more. Your stylist can provide a more accurate estimate during your consultation.

Do you offer consultations before booking a service?

Yes, we offer complimentary consultations to discuss your hair goals and determine the best approach for your desired look. You can book a consultation through our online booking system.
